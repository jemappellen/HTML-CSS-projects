ABOUT ME

Bonjour.

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Claude Monet, a painter dedicated to capturing the  beauty of light, movement, and nature.

As a pioneer of Impressionism, my work explores how color and atmosphere can transform the way we see the world.

Over the course of my career, I have developed a signature style that focuses on fleeting moments—whether it's the soft reflections of water lilies or the shifting tones of the Parisian skyline.

PROJECTS

Rouen Cathedral

A study of light, atmosphere, and architectural form. Between 1892 and 1894, I painted the cathedral’s façade at different times of the day, capturing the shifting interplay of sunlight, shadow, and color.

Water Lilies Series

Rather than focusing on a fixed perspective, I sought to immerse the viewer in an infinite, meditative space—where water, air, and light become indistinguishable. Through more than 250 paintings, I explored the ever-changing qualities of nature, from the delicate pastels of morning to the deep blues and golds of twilight.

Impression, Sunrise

Painted in the port of Le Havre, my hometown, this piece captures the soft glow of the rising sun as it dissolves into mist, blending sky and water into a delicate harmony of blues and oranges.
