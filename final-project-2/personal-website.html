About

Bonjour! I’m Claude Monet. You may know me as the father of Impressionism, a painter obsessed with capturing light, color, and fleeting moments. Whether it’s the serene beauty of my water lilies or the misty glow of a sunrise, my work aims to bring emotion and movement to every brushstroke. This site showcases my finest "projects," so take a stroll through my world of color and creativity!
